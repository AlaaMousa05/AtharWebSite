What is Athar ?

  • Athar is a website dedicated to meeting the needs of people in disaster-stricken countries facing famine and severe shortages of food and medicine.

  • Athar allows companies to build a database to facilitate reaching those in need and receiving aid requests.

  • Additionally, it focuses on collecting electronic donations and transferring them to individuals in need.
